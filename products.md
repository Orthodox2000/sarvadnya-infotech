# Sarvadnya Infotech - Product & Service Portfolio

Sarvadnya Infotech is a leading provider of Tally solutions, customizations, and enterprise services. Below is a comprehensive list of our offerings.

## Tally Core Products
Comprehensive business management software solutions for small to large enterprises.

- **TallyPrime Silver**: Single-user edition for standalone PCs. Ideal for small businesses.
- **TallyPrime Gold**: Unlimited multi-user edition for multiple PCs on a LAN. Best for growing businesses.
- **TallyPrime Server**: Enterprise-class product for medium and large businesses to improve data concurrency and speed.
- **TallyPrime Upgrades**: Seamless transition paths from Silver to Gold or older versions to the latest TallyPrime.

## Specialized Tally Services
- **Tally Software Service (TSS) Renewal**: Annual subscription to keep your Tally updated with the latest features, statutory changes, and remote access.
- **Tally Cloud Services**: Access your Tally data anytime, anywhere securely via the cloud.
- **Tally AMC Services**: Annual Maintenance Contracts for priority support and regular system health checks.

## Tally Customized Modules (Verticals)
Tailored solutions for specific industries to optimize their unique workflows.

- **Clearing & Forwarding (C&F) Agencies**: specialized module for logistics and agent management.
- **Housing Societies**: Management of maintenance, billing, and accounting for residential complexes.
- **Transport & Logistics**: Fleet management, trip sheets, and delivery tracking.
- **Garment Module**: Size, color, and fabric-wise inventory management.
- **Sales & Commission Module**: Track sales performance and automate complex commission structures.
- **Excel to Tally**: Bulk data import tools for migrating records from spreadsheets to Tally.
- **Business Boosters**: Add-on modules to enhance productivity and reporting.
